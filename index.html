My current projects

Here is a list of a few of my current projects I am working on:

Sakura Heavy Industries
Fictional Japanese corporation who specialise in aircraft such as the SHI-15, or SHI-32
Euravia / Euravia Weapon System
Euravia is a fictional French aerospace company, its name is similar to 'Panavia', and Euravia Weapon Systems is the weapons division of the company. I also used it for PropPunk for a weapons manufacturer

A few of my aircraft projects

SHI Raven II
Supersonic variable geometry bomber like the B-1 or Tu-160, but smaller than both, it offers a smaller profile and can go as fast as them but not in range sadly (currently that is)
SHI-32 Phantom Fox
Stealth fighter meant to compete with the YF-23 and YF-22 in the ATF program, a joint development of Rockwell International and Sakura Heavy Industries, though never chosen for the program it would be developed secretly by SHI.
Euravia E-Q89
Refueling drone designed and manufactured by Euravia as a one off to show drones can do aerial refuelling like the Boeing MQ-25 Stingray, only significantly more French and European
SHI Constilate
The Constilate (fake word, google thinks is constipation what is sad to think a bomber has that) It would have an internally designated by SHI as "Zero Two" a reference to Zero Two herself

As seen above are some of my current projects and they are in partial active development as I go from one to the other and back to the other, I never finish them as I always want to improve them like the Lexus LFA's development.
