Sakura Heavy Industries

Sakura Heavy Industries or better known as 'SHI' originated out of Japan back in 1932 under the guise to design and produce aircraft and tanks for the Imperial Japanese. In 1932 SHI was founded by [REDACTED], who was a very smart individuals who had help from their friends and over the years SHI grew large especially during the Second World War. During the Second World War, they created tanks on par to Shermans, though were not mass produced enough, they even had help from the [REDACTED] who sent some top people from Germany to a temp contract and those who came from Germany were kept even after the Second World War.

Sakura Heavy Industries' Divisions

SHI has a few divisions from its aerospace division to its weapons division.

Sakurain Advanced Defence
S.A.D was created back in 1951 as a subsidiary to sell aircraft or weapons to other nations such as Australia or Austria for example.
Sakurain Aerospace
S.A.D's Aerospace Division created in 1951 for military aircraft such as the SHI-53 VTOL, or the SHI-89 though that was never sold to other nations and was made under a different division.
